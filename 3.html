Scottish Water Telephone Support Guide

1. Introduction

Welcome to the Scottish Water telephone support team. This guide will help you provide excellent customer service and address common inquiries efficiently.

2. Call Handling Basics

  • Always greet the caller politely: "Thank you for calling Scottish Water. This is [Your Name]. How may I assist you today?"
  • Listen carefully to the customer's issue
  • Use the customer's name when appropriate
  • Speak clearly and at a moderate pace
  • Show empathy and understanding

3. Common Inquiries and Responses

3.1 Billing Issues

Access the billing system and verify customer details before discussing specifics. Offer payment plans if necessary.

3.2 Water Quality Concerns

Log the complaint, ask for specific details (color, odor, taste), and schedule a water quality test if needed.

3.3 Service Interruptions

Check the maintenance schedule and inform the customer of any planned work. For unexpected interruptions, create a service ticket.

4. Escalation Procedures

If unable to resolve an issue, follow these steps:

  1. Inform the customer you need to escalate the issue
  2. Contact your supervisor or the relevant department
  3. Provide the customer with a follow-up timeframe

6. Data Protection and Privacy

Always verify the caller's identity before discussing account details. Never share personal information with unauthorized parties.
