Scottish Water Phone Helper Guide

1. Welcome, Little Helper!

You're now a superhero for Scottish Water! Your job is to answer phones and help people with their water problems. It's like being a water detective!

2. How to Answer the Phone

  • Pick up the phone when it rings (it's like magic!)
  • Say: "Hello! This is [Your Name] from Scottish Water. How can I help you today?"
  • Listen carefully to what the person says
  • Speak clearly, like you're talking to your grandma
  • Be nice and friendly, even if the person sounds upset

3. Common Water Problems

3.1 If someone can't pay their water bill

Tell them: "Don't worry! We can help you figure out how to pay little by little."

3.2 If the water looks or tastes funny

Say: "Oh no! We'll send our water taste-testers to check it out."

3.3 If there's no water coming out of the tap

Tell them: "Our water wizards might be fixing the pipes. Let's check!"

4. When You Need Help

If you don't know what to do:

  1. Tell the person: "I need to ask my super-smart helper for advice."
  2. Press the big red button to call your grown-up helper
  3. Tell the person you'll call them back soon

  • Grown-up Helper: Ask your team leader!

6. Keeping Secrets

Remember, people's information is like a secret. Don't tell anyone else!

If you forget anything, ask your grown-up helper. You're doing great, water superhero!
